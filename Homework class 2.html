About My School

I got to Canterbury Girls' Secondary College. My school is an all girls school and is from grades 7 to 12. I am in year 8.

My school has a string orchestra where I play the violin after school every Thursday. In my school, we learn subjects such as English, maths, science, food tech, personal development, P.E., drama, French, georgraphy and history.

My school starts at 8:50 and ends at 3:05 everyday. We have breakfast club on Thursday mornings. We have 4 terms in a year and each term last for around 9-12 weeks. After every term, we have a 2 week holiday, but after term 4, we have a 5 week holiday.
